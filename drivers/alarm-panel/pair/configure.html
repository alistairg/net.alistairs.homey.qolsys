Pair Your Qolsys Panel

This app pairs Homey with your Qolsys IQ Panel as a virtual IQ Remote. Pairing is a one-time certificate exchange — once complete, Homey reconnects automatically every time the panel is online.

Heads up: some panels enforce a limit on virtual remotes (3 on IQ Panel 2/2+, 8 on IQ Panel 4 / IQ Pro), and freeing a slot may require an alarm-company reset. Make sure you have a free slot before continuing.
